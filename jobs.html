Front-End Web Developer

Reference Number: FE101

Salary: $75,000 – $90,000 per year

Reporting Line: Reports to the Creative Director

Short Description

We are seeking a Front-End Web Developer to create responsive and visually engaging websites for a range of client projects. The successful applicant will work closely with designers and content creators to deliver high-quality web experiences.

Key Responsibilities

  1. Develop responsive website layouts using HTML5 and CSS3.
  2. Translate design mock-ups into functional and accessible web pages.
  3. Maintain visual consistency across multiple client websites.
  4. Test and refine pages to improve usability and presentation.
  5. Work closely with designers and project team members to meet deadlines.

Essential Requirements

  • Strong skills in HTML5 and CSS3.
  • Understanding of responsive web design principles.
  • Ability to follow design specifications accurately.
  • Good communication and teamwork skills.
  • Attention to detail and commitment to quality.

Preferable Requirements

  • Experience working on client-based web projects.
  • Knowledge of accessibility and usability best practices.
  • Familiarity with branding and digital media workflows.

UI/UX Designer

Reference Number: UX202

Salary: $70,000 – $85,000 per year

Reporting Line: Reports to the Design Lead

Short Description

We are looking for a UI/UX Designer to help create engaging and user-friendly digital experiences for our clients. This role focuses on interface design, visual communication, and improving how users interact with websites and digital content.

Key Responsibilities

  1. Design clean and attractive website interfaces for client projects.
  2. Create wireframes, layout concepts, and visual design elements.
  3. Work with developers to ensure designs are implemented correctly.
  4. Support branding projects with consistent visual design choices.
  5. Review and improve the usability of website layouts and page content.

Essential Requirements

  • Strong visual design and layout skills.
  • Understanding of user experience and interface design principles.
  • Ability to communicate design ideas clearly.
  • Good organisational and time management skills.
  • Ability to work effectively in a collaborative creative team.

Preferable Requirements

  • Experience with branding or digital content design.
  • Understanding of how design choices affect front-end development.
  • Previous experience in a creative agency environment.
